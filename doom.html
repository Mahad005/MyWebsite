Doom Summary

Doom is a first person shooter that first came out in 1993. It was developed by Id Software for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System & has been released onto multiple different consoles. It's one of the most influencial games of all time. Due to how popular the first game was, Doom got multiple sequels, ports, rereleases, & expansions.

Story

The series is about a space marine with no name(Usually called Doomguy) on Mars killing demons from Hell using a vast variaty of weapons. There isn't much lore in the series, but the series makes up for it with its gameplay.

Gameplay

The series is a first-person shooter, where you have to shoot anything that moves. Doom is one of the biggest influences for the genre as a whole. You go through multiple levels trying to find the exit while shooting demons. You can get many types of guns as you progress, like a shotgun, plasma rifle, chaingun, rocket launcher, & more. You can also pick up powerups that can make you go invisible, give you extra health, or let you kill thing in one punch. There are also many different types of enemies, like infected soldiers, alien-like demons that shoot fireballs, fiery floating skulls, Satan himself, etc.
